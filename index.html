WHITEBOX LEARNING: PROSTHETICS

In Whitebox Learning, we designed a prosthetic arm to throw a ball a specific distance, and made adjustments to different variables, such as the lengths of each piece and the angle at which the object was thrown, to get the ball right on the target. After adjusting our designs for different distances, we constructed our own prosthetic arms and threw a ping pong ball into a bucket!

SKETCHUP 3D MODELING

In this project, we used Sketchup to make a ring that would help with everyday tasks, such as holding a pencil and drumming! For my project, I made a ring that would hold multiple dog leashes! VEX ROBOTICS: SWEEP AWAY

In this project, we built a robot and programmed it to play a game! In small groups, we built the robot itself, then broke off into even smaller groups to prepare for the challenge! Finally, we set up an arena in the auditorium and competed against our classmates! My robot, Thai, lost in the championship round by one point!
